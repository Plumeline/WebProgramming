Where we act

The places where Good4Health acts are numerous. Our actions can be found in Bolivia, in Angola, in Niger and in North Corea.
Our actions include, but are not limited, to creation of wells to provide safe water to population, distribution and administration of medicine,
and courses to teach basic hygiene reflexes to people. Our numbers speak for themselves : many lives can be saved simply by giving material and
proper education.
